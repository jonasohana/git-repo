Three-toed Sloth

Sloths prefer sleeping while curled into a ball in the fork of a tropical tree. They also like to sleep hanging by their claws from tree branches

  • Scientific Name: Bradypus (slow foot)
  • Location: Central and South America
  • Size: 23-27 inches (58 to 68 cm)
  • Weight: 17.5-18.75 lbs (~ 8 kg)

Pale-throated sloths have a rounded head with a blunt nose and small external ears. The limbs are long and weak, with the arms being nearly twice the length of the hindlimbs.
